Everest the cat

Cat is a carnivore catching smaller mam-mals such as mice and other rodents, birds, insects and bugs, lizards even snakes. Are good for keeping as pet.

  • Scientific name: felis silvestris
  • Material: furr
  • Size: few kilo's
  • Speciality: hunting

Tiny furrball colored in white with black-grey striped areas on head, back, legs and tail. four legs with pillow-paws and sharp claws. Grums his moustaches daily and cleans himself thoroughly with his tongue. Great climber and experienced hunter.
